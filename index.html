Design and Implementation of Robust PID Control

Implementation of three different strategies of Robust Control namely : Flat Phase control, Variable Structure PID and a combination of Pole placement and Linear Quadratic Regulator. We interfaced the controller with a two-tank system and compared results with Ziegler Nichols Controller, proving our controller to be superior during experimentation.
